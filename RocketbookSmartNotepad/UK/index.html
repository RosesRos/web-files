FAQs

Is the Rocketbook notepad waterproof?

The Rocketbook itself is not waterproof, although the pages are water-resistant. It's important to keep the notepad away from excessive water to avoid damage

Can I use any pen with Rocketbook?

No, Rocketbook is designed to be used with Pilot FriXion pens, markers, and highlighters. These pens use erasable ink that works with the Rocketbook pages

How does the Rocketbook app work?

The Rocketbook app allows you to quickly scan and digitize your handwritten notes. You can save and organize these notes in cloud services like Google Drive, Dropbox, Evernote, and more

How do you erase Rocketbook pages?

You can erase your notes with a damp cloth. For the best results, it’s recommended to wait 15 seconds for the ink to dry before erasing

Can I share my Rocketbook
notes with others?

Yes, once your notes are digitized using the Rocketbook app, you can easily share them via email or upload them to various cloud services, making collaboration and sharing simple
