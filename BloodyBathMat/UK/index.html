FAQs

Is the color change permanent?

No, the color change is not permanent. The mat will revert to its original state once it's dry. The color-changing effect can be used repeatedly.

Will the red color stain my floor or tub?

No, the red color will not stain your floor or tub. The color-change is contained within the fabric of the mat and does not transfer.

What size is the Bloody Bath Mat?

Please check the specific product listing for the exact dimensions as sizes may vary. Generally, it's designed to fit standard bathroom spaces.

Can the Bloody Bath Mat be used outside the bathroom?

While it's primarily designed for bathroom use due to its absorbent qualities, you can use it in other areas if you want to add a spooky touch. Just note that it needs water to activate the color change.

How do I care for and maintain the mat?

To care for the mat, machine wash it in cold water on a gentle cycle and let it air dry. Avoid using bleach or high heat.
