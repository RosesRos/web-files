FAQs

How often should I use the serum for the best results?

For optimal results, it is typically recommended to use the serum daily, following the instructions provided with the product

Can the serum be used on dental work such as veneers, crowns, or fillings?

The serum is designed for use on natural teeth. It might not have the same whitening effect on dental work like veneers, crowns, or fillings. It's always best to consult your dentist for advice

How long does it take to see results from the V34 HiSmile Teeth Whitening Serum?

Results can vary depending on your initial tooth color and the type of staining. most users see noticeable results after the first applications

How long does one bottle of the serum last?

The duration a bottle lasts depends on your usage frequency. Typically, a bottle can last for several weeks when used as directed

Can the serum remove all types of stains from the teeth?

The serum is effective in removing many common surface stains caused by food, drink, and tobacco. However, it may be less effective on discoloration caused by certain medications or dental trauma
